Source

The source code is currently hosted on GitHub. Don't be surprised, this repository really contains mostly patches. In order to build Wine Staging you'll additionally need upstream Wine. The repository also contains a couple of helper scripts to simplify the steps of applying all or a subset of the patches. Detailed instructions how to build Wine Staging are available in our packaging instructions.

Contributing

We are also happy about contributions. If you for example spend a lot of time figuring out how to solve a specific issue, but have trouble with your patch getting accepted upstream, then we can also take a look at it and will add it if it passes our review. We will not accept hacks for single applications and all patches must at least have a minimum standard quality. If your patch does not fulfill these quality requirements, we will tell you about the remaining problems, and give you a chance to correct them. We are open for all other kind of patches and in contrast to upstream Wine, we also accept bigger work-in-progress patchsets. You can find more about this in our wiki.
